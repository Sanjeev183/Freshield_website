About Freshield

Founded with a vision to revolutionize the cleaning industry, Freshield has been at the forefront of innovation in phenyl and disinfectant manufacturing for over a decade. Our commitment to quality, sustainability, and customer satisfaction has made us a trusted name in cleaning solutions across the region.

We believe that a clean environment is fundamental to health and well-being. That's why we invest heavily in research and development to create products that not only clean effectively but also protect the environment and promote healthier living spaces.

Our state-of-the-art manufacturing facility employs advanced quality control measures and follows strict environmental guidelines. Every product that leaves our factory meets the highest standards of quality and safety.
